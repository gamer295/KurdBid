# 📲 How to Install as an App (No Computer Needed)

Since you don't have a computer right now, the **easiest and 100% free way** to get this on your phone is to use it as a **PWA (Progressive Web App)**. It will look and act exactly like a real app.

### For Android (Samsung, Huawei, Pixel, etc.)
1. Open your website in **Google Chrome**.
2. Tap the **three dots (⋮)** in the top-right corner.
3. Select **"Install App"** (or "Add to Home Screen").
4. Confirm by tapping **"Add"**.
5. The **KurdBid** icon will now appear on your home screen!

### For iPhone (iOS)
1. Open your website in **Safari**.
2. Tap the **Share icon** (the square with an arrow pointing up) at the bottom.
3. Scroll down and tap **"Add to Home Screen"**.
4. Tap **"Add"** in the top-right corner.
5. The **KurdBid** icon will now appear on your home screen!

### 💡 Why this is better than an APK?
- **No Paywalls:** You don't have to pay build services like VoltBuilder.
- **No Apple Fee:** You don't need a $99/year Apple Developer account.
- **Always Updated:** Every time I make a change here and you push to GitHub, your phone app updates automatically!
- **Fast:** It takes 2 seconds to install.
